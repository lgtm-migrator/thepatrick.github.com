The Decade Project

About

A complete rip of off of the Ten Years of My Life project that started roughly 6 months before The Decade Project, - having stumbled upon it and followed it, I wanted to do something similar. It was my third year of university, and already planning on relocating to Canada, I felt like it was a good opportunity to try and capture what my twenties were. If I was smarter I would have waited for my 20th birthday, and maybe kept it going until my 30th. I didn't.
