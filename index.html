About me

I am just somebody living in Sydney, Australia.

I currently manage the team that setups and maintains the infrastructure that sees routine traffic spikes above 85,000 requests per second and generates 5TB/day of logs. I'm hiring.

I love empowering people to design, build and operate scalable, high throughput services. I have a strong interest in improving developer experience - especially through automation of development and deployment processes.

I am interested in different programming languages - I have written code in JavaScript, TypeScript, Go, C++, C#, Python, Rust, Scala, Swift and Objective-C. I have wrangled infrastructure with CloudFormation, Terraform, Pulumi, Ansible, Puppet and Chef. I have wrangled containers with Kubernetes on GKE and EKS. I consider running a baremetal k8s cluster fun to do at home.

I like doing AV things at events, see Two Pats.

Sometimes I talk at conferences or meetups. When I do I put an entry on the talks page, with links to slides & code.
